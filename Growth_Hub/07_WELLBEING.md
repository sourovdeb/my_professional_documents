# 07 — Wellbeing & Understanding Your Condition

**Goal: protect the person, because the person runs the whole system. And understand your condition from official sources — never folklore.**

> This file is for self-education and self-care only. It is **not** medical advice and replaces nothing. Every treatment, dose, and diagnosis decision stays with **Dr. Pauvert and your monthly psychiatric review.** Nothing here invents a clinical instruction.

## Take care of yourself first — the non-negotiables

These already live in your [Daily Living Strategy](../personal_doc_extracted/DAILY_LIVING_STRATEGY.md). They matter more than any writing goal. If a writing day threatens an anchor, the anchor wins.

**The four anchors (every day, any state):**
1. **Fixed wake time**, 7 days a week — the single strongest mood regulator you have.
2. **Meds + water + daylight** on waking.
3. **One real meal** at a roughly fixed time (a rhythm cue, not just food).
4. **One-line capture:** "Today I am ___ . Today the one thing is ___ ."

If all four happen, the day counts — even if you wrote nothing. Defending the rhythm is what prevents the next episode. That is the job.

**For the writing life specifically:**
- Sleep beats output. Never trade a wind-down for "one more paragraph." Hypomania will try to; the hard stop in [06_CONSISTENCY](06_CONSISTENCY.md) is there for exactly this.
- Don't read your own comments or analytics late at night. Batch that for a stable, daylight window.
- Rest is part of the plan, not a reward you earn. A crash day is survival mode, and survival mode is legitimate.

## What to learn about your condition (official sources only)

You carry documented **bipolar I, ADHD, depression, and clinical insomnia.** Learning the mechanics — from real clinical bodies — turns a scary, shapeless thing into a system you can route around. That is empowering, and it makes you a more honest writer on the subject.

**Bipolar disorder**
- **NIMH — Bipolar Disorder:** symptoms, treatment, and self-management (routine, mood charting, avoiding alcohol/drugs). <https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/publications/bipolar-disorder>
- **NHS — Bipolar disorder:** plain-language overview, living-with guidance. <https://www.nhs.uk/mental-health/conditions/bipolar-disorder/>
- **HAS (Haute Autorité de Santé, France):** official French clinical guidance — <https://www.has-sante.fr>

**ADHD (adult)**
- **NICE / NHS — ADHD:** diagnosis and management. <https://www.nhs.uk/conditions/attention-deficit-hyperactivity-disorder-adhd/>
- **NIMH — ADHD.** <https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/attention-deficit-hyperactivity-disorder-adhd>

**Insomnia / sleep**
- **NHS — Insomnia** and CBT-I (the first-line, non-drug treatment): <https://www.nhs.uk/conditions/insomnia/>
- Your referred **polysomnography (sleep study)** — chase it; untreated apnea sabotages mood and focus.

**The evidence your strategy already rests on** (worth reading once, properly):
- **Social Rhythm Therapy (Frank)** — why the fixed wake time anchors mood.
- **Behavioural activation** — why small actions lift low mood.
- **Interest-based attention (Dodson)** — why ADHD focus follows interest, not importance — and why writing on your real obsessions works.

**How to read these well:** trust `.gov`, `.nhs.uk`, NIMH, HAS, and peer-reviewed sources. Treat forums and influencers as *experience*, not fact. When you cite anything in your writing, link the official source — that honesty is your brand.

## The French system that supports you (official, and it unlocks money + protection)

These are the live administrative unlocks from your plan. Each one is real, legal support you're entitled to. Track each to "done" with the next date visible.

| What | Unlocks | Official source |
|------|---------|-----------------|
| **ALD** (Affection Longue Durée), via Dr. Pauvert | Covers the cost of psychiatric care | [Ameli](https://www.ameli.fr) |
| **RQTH** (Travailleur Handicapé) | Protected employment + AGEFIPH funding + auto-entrepreneur support | [Mon Parcours Handicap](https://www.monparcourshandicap.gouv.fr/aides/la-reconnaissance-de-la-qualite-de-travailleur-handicape-rqth) |

| **AGEFIPH** | Funded help to find/keep private-sector work | [AGEFIPH](https://www.agefiph.fr) |
| **Cap emploi** | Specialist disability employment support | via MDPH / [Mon Parcours Handicap](https://www.monparcourshandicap.gouv.fr) |

Handicap *psychique* (bipolar, depression) **is** recognised by the MDPH — you qualify to apply. This is not charity; it is a legal framework built for exactly your situation.

## If a crisis hits

- **France — 3114**: national suicide-prevention line, free, 24/7.
- **SAMU — 15** for a medical emergency; **112** EU-wide.
- Keep Dr. Pauvert's number and your monthly-review date on the visible anchor card.

You do not have to earn rest, and you do not have to do this alone. The system carries the day the person can't — and on the days it can't, the people and services above are part of the system too.
