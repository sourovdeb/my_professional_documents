# Exposure Research — Reaching Readers Without Social Media (2026-07-14)

Scope: mental-health writing, English teaching (CELTA/IELTS/TOEIC/Business), general blogging. Excludes Instagram/TikTok/X/Facebook by design — email, print, communities, and direct outreach only.

## 1. Mental-Health Writing
- **Platforms:** Substack (email-first; Recommendations = main growth lever), The Mighty (submit essay), MentalHealth.com contributor program, Open Minds Quarterly (print lit journal).
- **Audience:** 25–45, EN-speaking US/UK/EU; The Mighty skews lived-experience/disability community. FR/Réunion bilingual angle is untested — no confirmed French mental-health essay outlet found.
- **Channels:** owned email newsletter; pitch essays via Submittable to niche outlets; guest on small (1K–25K listener) podcasts.
- **Unconventional:** print zines (2026 zine revival) sold at indie book fairs; postcards/flyers left at therapy practices & wellness clinics with newsletter QR code; recommendation-swaps with 4–5 peer wellness newsletters.
- **Next steps:** pitch 3–5 essays to The Mighty + Open Minds Quarterly; start newsletter with a swap circle.

## 2. English Teaching (CELTA / IELTS / TOEIC / Business English)
- **Platforms:** TEFL.com/TEFL.net job boards, TESOL France (Paris, IATEFL-affiliated, monthly SIGs + annual Colloquium), IH World teacher hub, exam-prep marketplaces (italki, Preply, Talaera, Lingoda).
- **Audience:** global CELTA/TEFL teachers + corporate learners; TOEIC dominates French corporate testing — CCI (Chambre de Commerce) L&D departments are a direct target.
- **Channels:** TESOL France membership + SIG events (in-person network); direct outreach to local language schools and CCI/HR L&D contacts.
- **Unconventional:** printed one-pagers at CCI/business events; mailers to HR/L&D departments pitching corporate CELTA-led workshops; cross-list on 2–3 marketplaces with exam-prep tags (directory discovery, not social).
- **Next steps:** join TESOL France, attend one SIG; build TEFL.com + one marketplace profile; cold-email 5 local CCI/L&D contacts with a PDF one-pager.
- **Gap:** no unified Alliance Française/CCI teacher job board exists — each location lists separately; treat as direct-outreach targets.

## 3. General Blogging/Essays
- **Platforms:** Substack (primary), Medium (secondary cross-post, declining reach), Scribophile/Reedsy for craft community (not audience-building).
- **Audience:** 25–55, educated, EN-speaking, US/UK-heavy with expat/bilingual overlap given France base.
- **Channels:** owned newsletter; Substack Recommendation swaps (strongest documented non-social growth mechanic in 2026); local médiathèque/library "local author" placement (well-documented in US, unverified for France/Réunion).
- **Unconventional:** self-published zine/chapbook distributed at indie book fairs; hand-addressed postcard mailers to local cafés/cultural centers announcing the newsletter (postcard response rates cited 5–9x digital); weekly/biweekly cadence — monthly posting drops out of newsletter recommendation ranking.
- **Next steps:** consolidate one email-first newsletter as the hub for all three niches; recruit 4–5 peers for a recommendation-swap circle; contact local médiathèque about an author event.
