# Contact and Outreach System
**Job contacts, writing partners, and useful people — quality over quantity**

---

## Finding Jobs — Three Channels

### 1. Automated Job Search (daily)
```bash
python AUTOMATION_TOOLS/job_search.py
```
Review the CSV each morning. Apply to 3 maximum per day.

### 2. Direct Outreach to Organisations
Target organisations that need English training:
- Aviation companies in La Réunion (Air Austral, Air Madagascar)
- Hospitals and medical centres (medical English)
- Hotels and tourism operators (hospitality English)
- Chambers of commerce (business English)
- OPCO-funded training organisations

Template: see `AUTOMATION_TOOLS/AUTOMATION_GUIDE.md` → Contact Templates

### 3. LinkedIn Profile Activation
- Post one short piece per week (LinkedIn native article or short post)
- Connect with CELTA trainers, EFL teachers, and language school directors in France and Indian Ocean
- Comment on posts by Pôle Emploi, OPCO, and Qualiopi-certified training bodies

## Finding Writing Partners

**What to look for:**
- Writers with lived experience of mental health, disability, or neurodivergence
- Expat writers in non-English countries writing in English
- Education writers — especially those critical of institutional gatekeeping
- Language teachers and polyglots who write about the experience

**Where to find them:**
- Medium: search tags #ADHD #bipolar #expat #EFL #CELTA
- Substack: Notes section, search by topic
- Twitter/X: #ActuallyADHD #WritingCommunity #BipolarTwitter
- LinkedIn: `CELTA` + `writer` or `blogger`

**First contact rule:** comment genuinely before connecting. No pitch on first contact. Read one full piece first.

## Useful Organisations and Contacts

### Disability and Mental Health in Education (France)
- AGEFIPH — disability employment support
- RQTH status — get this formalised, unlocks job protections
- MDPH (Maison Départementale des Personnes Handicapées) — La Réunion branch
- Cap Emploi — supported employment for disabled workers

### English Teaching / TEFL Networks
- TESOL France — annual conference, networking
- British Council France — accreditation and professional development
- Cambridge English — assessor and examiner networks
- IDP Education France — IELTS specialist contacts

### Bipolar / ADHD Support (France)
- ARGOS 2001 — French bipolar patient association
- HyperSupers TDAH France — ADHD association
- UNAFAM — family and patients mental health

### Blogging / Writing Exposure
- The Mighty (themighty.com) — mental health writing, open submissions
- Psychology Today (psychologytoday.com) — bloggers programme
- Elephant Journal (elephantjournal.com) — mindfulness and personal growth
- Understood.org — ADHD and learning differences audience

## Weekly Rhythm (Sustainable)

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Monday | Run job_search.py, review CSV, send 3 applications |
| Tuesday | Write and publish one blog post |
| Wednesday | 5 genuine comments on writing platforms |
| Thursday | One LinkedIn post or article |
| Friday | Review applications sent, follow up where needed |
| Weekend | Rest. Reading counts. Writing in a notebook counts. |

Crisis days: skip the action, not the rest.
