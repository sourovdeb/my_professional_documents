# EU Education Opportunities - New Ideas (Deep Brainstorm)

## Frame
- Subject: Hidden education opportunities across EU member states
- Goal: Improve discoverability and access to underpublicized programs
- Constraints: GDPR compliance, 27 fragmented systems, language barriers
- Horizons: Immediate (3m), 1 year, 5+ years
- Success: User adoption, opportunity conversion rate, data accuracy, scalability

## Perspective Rotation (Top Idea: EU Education USB)
- End User (Rural EU student): Needs offline access, simple interface, trustworthy sources
- Adversary (Established education platform): Will argue this duplicates their services, may lobby against
- Regulator (EU Commission): Must ensure GDPR compliance for data on USB, no tracking
- Financier (EU grant): Wants measurable impact on education access inequality
- Operator: Needs regular USB updates, distribution logistics, usage tracking

## Second-Order Effects
- Short-term: Students in low-connectivity areas gain access to opportunities
- Long-term: Reduced education inequality across EU regions
- Then: Increased student mobility and cross-border education
- And then: Pressure on institutions to improve opportunity transparency
