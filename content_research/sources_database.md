## How to Use This

1. **Pick a topic you want to write about**
2. **Find the official source(s)** from this table
3. **Read and take notes** in `research_log.md`
4. **Cite with the link** in your essay
5. **Add new sources** when you find them

---

## How to Verify a Source

✓ **Good sources have:**
- Government (.gov), educational (.edu), or established organization domain
- Clear author and date
- Citations/references
- Transparency about funding
- No ads disguised as content

✗ **Avoid:**
- Medium, Substack, personal blogs
- "Top 10 listicles"
- Marketing content (HubSpot, Neil Patel)
- Outdated info (>3 years old for current topics)
- Anything without attribution
