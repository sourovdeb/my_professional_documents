# 02 — Tools (free / open-source)

Everything here is free or has a real free tier. The rule: **fewer tools, used daily.**
Pick one per row, learn it once, stop shopping. Tool-shopping is procrastination wearing
a productive costume — dangerous on an ADHD brain.

## Write & think
| Need | Tool | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Notes + writing + "second brain" | **Obsidian** (free) or **Logseq** (open-source) | Plain `.md` files = same format as this repo. Your board, your ideas, your drafts in one place. Works offline. |
| Distraction-free drafting | **Obsidian** focus mode, or **FocusWriter** (open-source) | Full-screen, no UI, just words. |
| Grammar / clarity | **LanguageTool** (open-source, free tier) | Self-hostable; better than Grammarly for French + English. |
| Readability check | **Hemingway** (free web) | Flags passive voice and long sentences. Helps you stay active-voice and human. |
| Spaced learning | **Anki** (open-source) | For studying your condition, languages, teaching vocab. |

## Design & visuals (make it look professional)
| Need | Tool | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Post images, CV, one-pagers | **Canva** (free tier) | Fast, templated, good enough for everything social. |
| Pro-grade design, fully free | **Penpot** or **GIMP** + **Inkscape** (all open-source) | When you want full control and zero cost. |
| Screenshots / annotated images | **Flameshot** (open-source) | One key, annotate, done. |
| Diagrams | **Excalidraw** (open-source) | Hand-drawn-style diagrams for your "system" essays. |
| Stock images, free to use | **Unsplash / Pexels** | Always check the licence; both are free for commercial use. |

## Publish & automate writing
| Need | Tool | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Newsletter / blog home | **Ghost** (open-source) / **Substack** | See file 01. |
| Schedule social posts | **Buffer** (free tier) or **Postiz** (open-source) | Write a batch on a good day, drip it out all week. Protects your streak on crash days. |
| Cross-post automatically | your own **Email_Extension** + n8n (see file 03) | Recycle what you already built. |

## Make life comfortable (the quiet productivity layer)
| Need | Tool | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Password & login pain | **Bitwarden** (open-source, free) | One less thing for working memory to hold. |
| Time-block / timer | **Pomofocus** (free) or a kitchen timer | Pairs with the 25-min writing block. |
| Read-it-later | **Wallabag** (open-source) | Capture sources without falling down a rabbit hole now. |
| Local AI assistant | **Ollama** (open-source) + Mistral/Llama | You already use this in your extension. Free, private, runs offline. |
| Voice-to-text drafting | **Whisper** (open-source, OpenAI) | On low-focus days, *talk* your 500 words and clean them up. Huge for ADHD. |
| Calendar / reminders | **Proton Calendar** or Google Calendar | One calendar. Alarms for meds and ship-time (anchors from your strategy). |

## The minimum viable stack (if you only set up five things)
1. **Obsidian** — ideas, drafts, this repo, your board. One place.
2. **Substack** — publish + audience.
3. **Whisper** (or phone voice notes) — draft by talking on hard days.
4. **Canva** — make it look good.
5. **Ollama** — your private AI co-writer, free, already in your workflow.

Set these up once, on a stable day. Then stop. The tools are not the work — the 500
words are the work.
