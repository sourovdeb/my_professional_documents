# GROWTH — the engine

This is the active layer. The goal is simple and stacked in order:

1. **Write every day** so my story and philosophy get heard.
2. That writing builds **exposure**, which builds **contacts**.
3. Contacts and a public body of work build **income** — jobs, clients, partners.
4. All of it runs on systems that survive a bad mental-health day.

Read the five files in order, but you only ever *act* on one thing a day.

| File | Answers |
|---|---|
| **01_WRITE_AND_EXPOSURE.md** | Where do I publish? What do I write? How do 500 words a day become an audience? |
| **02_TOOLS.md** | Free / open-source tools to write, design, and make life comfortable. |
| **03_AUTOMATION.md** | Open-source automation: find jobs, draft emails, find like-minded writers and partners — so I only have to write. |
| **04_CONTACTS.md** | Who to reach, how to reach them, how to keep the relationship warm. |
| **05_CONSISTENCY_AND_SELFCARE.md** | The productivity technique, staying consistent, taking care of myself, and what to learn about my condition. |

## The one rule

**500 words, published, once a day. Everything else is optional.**

If only that happens, the day counts. This mirrors the crash-day rule in my Daily
Living Strategy: the system is designed to run on the worst version of me, so it never
needs motivation to execute.

## The first 30 days (do this, ignore everything else)

- **Days 1–3:** Pick ONE home base (see 01 — I recommend Ghost or Substack to start) and ONE distribution channel (LinkedIn). Don't optimise. Just open the accounts.
- **Days 4–7:** Publish 4 short posts pulled straight from `Story_of_Sourov` and `Biography_and_Medical` — my story already exists, I'm just cutting it into 500-word pieces.
- **Week 2:** Keep publishing daily. Set up the writing tools in 02. Stop redesigning.
- **Week 3:** Turn on ONE automation from 03 (start with job alerts — lowest effort, highest payoff). Keep writing.
- **Week 4:** Reach out to 3 people from 04. Keep writing.

After 30 days I'll have ~25 published pieces, a tool set, one live automation, and three
real conversations. That is the whole game. Then repeat.

## Track it (one line a day)

Keep a file `GROWTH/log.md`. One line per day:
```
2026-05-30 | published: "title" | reached out: name | felt: 6/10
```
That's the only metric. Streak of published days > everything else.
