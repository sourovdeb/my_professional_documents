# Risk Mitigation Strategy

## Risk Categories

### Regulatory Risks
- **Bengali**: Ministry of Information & Broadcasting (MIB) licensing requirements
- **EU**: General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) compliance
- **Migration**: Legal liability for providing migration advice

**Mitigation**: Consult legal experts before launch, maintain compliance documentation, include disclaimers

### Financial Risks
- **Bengali**: Low listener base, insufficient sponsorship
- **EU**: Low user adoption, high maintenance costs
- **Migration**: High SMS costs, unsustainable funding model

**Mitigation**: Diversify funding sources (grants 60%, sponsorships 20%, donations 10%, crowdfunding 10%), set budget caps, implement cost controls

### Technical Risks
- **Bengali**: Streaming downtime, equipment failure
- **EU**: Data accuracy issues, scraping reliability
- **Migration**: Platform scalability, data security

**Mitigation**: Use reliable cloud services (DigitalOcean, AWS), implement backup systems, monitor uptime

### Content Risks
- **Bengali**: Copyright infringement, controversial content
- **EU**: False opportunity listings, outdated information
- **Migration**: Incorrect legal advice, unverified sources

**Mitigation**: Implement review processes, use verified primary sources, establish correction policies

### Reputational Risks
- **Bengali**: Perceived bias, community backlash
- **EU**: Accusations of promoting unfair advantages
- **Migration**: Legal challenges from governments

**Mitigation**: Clear disclaimers, transparent methodology, balanced representation

- Monthly risk review meetings
- Quarterly external audit of high-risk areas
- Immediate reporting of any legal threats
