# Ideas Inbox — one place, capture now, sort later

*The single rule: every idea goes here the instant it arrives, unsorted. You sort on a stable day, never at the moment of capture. ADHD + working memory means an idea not written is an idea gone.*

## How to use it

1. Idea arrives → drop one line below under **Inbox**. Don't judge it. Don't format it. Just catch it.
2. On your weekly stable day → move the good ones up to the **Topic Bank** (`01_WRITE/TOPIC_BANK.md`) or into a draft.
3. Delete the dead ones without guilt. A short inbox is a healthy inbox.

> Capturing on your phone? Dictate it. Speech is faster than your inner critic.
