# Exposure Without Social Media — Mental Health, English Teaching, Blogging

*Complements [EXPOSURE_PLAYBOOK.md](EXPOSURE_PLAYBOOK.md). This version assumes the reader is NOT on Instagram/TikTok/X/LinkedIn — reach them where they actually are.*

## 1. Mental health storytelling
- **Audience:** adults 30–55 managing a diagnosis long-term (not the 18–25 TikTok mental-health crowd); Réunion/France + global English-reading diaspora.
- **Channels:** NAMI (In Our Own Voice program, Share-Your-Story submissions), local peer-support groups (bipolar/ADHD/CPTSD), therapist waiting-room handouts, hospital/clinic community boards, podcasts (audio needs no social account), disability-rights press (Réunion/French local papers).
- **Direct outreach:** offer your essays free to therapists/counselors to hand to clients; pitch guest posts to *NAMI blog*, *The Mighty*, disability-focused outlets — these route traffic via search/email, not feeds.

## 2. English teaching (CELTA/TEFL)
- **Audience:** language schools, HR/L&D managers, adult learners prepping IELTS/TOEIC; Réunion + mainland France + international schools market.
- **Channels:** IATEFL and TESOL (associations, Special/Interest Groups, regional conferences — the field's real network); British Council teacher network; Chambers of Commerce; direct email/post to schools (see job-leads sheet).
- **Direct outreach:** physical CV/portfolio drops at language schools; presenting at a local IATEFL/TESOL chapter meetup; teacher-association newsletters (not algorithmic, opt-in email).

## 3. General blogging
- **Audience:** search-driven readers (Google), people who read via RSS/email — an aging but loyal segment that never left email.
- **Channels:** SEO (own domain, Yoast/Rank Math) — search doesn't require social media at all; email newsletter (Substack/own list); newsletter swaps and sponsorships with related creators; syndication to Medium/Substack for reach without needing followers.
- **Direct outreach:** guest posts on established blogs in your niches; local press for the CELTA/disability angle.

## Unconventional tactics (all three)
- **Physical mail:** postcards to schools/clinics announcing new work — 2026 data shows direct mail still outperforms crowded digital feeds for attention (Vomela, PFL 2026).
- **Newsletter strategy:** email remains the top asset you own — 2025 data: ~41% average open rates, well above social reach. Host past issues on your own domain for SEO compounding.
- **Community partnerships:** launch parties/events requiring an email signup (the GIST newsletter got 1,000 subscribers in one night this way); newsletter swaps with adjacent creators; co-hosted talks with local associations.
