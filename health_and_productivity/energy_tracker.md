# Daily Energy Tracker

**Purpose:** Track energy levels, not productivity. Different energy levels = different task types.

---

## How to Use

Each morning, rate yourself 1-10:
- **9-10**: Hypomanic. Write, create, reach out. Also: check impulse control.
- **7-8**: Normal high. Excellent for writing, research, outreach
- **5-6**: Normal. Can do most things, gets tired after 2 hours
- **3-4**: Tired. Use templates, edit existing work, organize
- **1-2**: Depressive. Rest. No expectations. Push drafts to WP if you can.

## Triggers & Patterns

What tends to spike your energy (good and bad)?

**Manic triggers:**
- Late night social media
- Too much caffeine
- Sleep disruption

**Depressive triggers:**
- Isolation
- Rejection (job, email)
- Medication changes

**Stabilizing practices:**
- 8 hours sleep
- 30 min walk
- Limit news/notifications
- One meal with someone

## Weekly Review Template (Sunday 6 PM)

Answer these every Sunday:

1. **Best moment this week?** (energy, writing, or personal)
2. **Hardest moment?** (what triggered the low?)
3. **What helped?** (medication, friends, structure, rest)
4. **For next week:** One thing to do more of, one thing to do less of
5. **Doctor check-in needed?** (Yes/No)
