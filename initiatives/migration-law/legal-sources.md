### Key Cases by Topic

#### Asylum Procedure
- **EU**: C-175/08 (Abdulla), C-69/10 (Brahim Samba Diouf)
- **Germany**: BVerwG 1 C 4.07 (Safe country of origin)
- **France**: CE, 13 juillet 2012, n 335033 (Dublin regulation)

#### Detention
- **EU**: C-61/11 PPU (El Dridi), C-573/14 (Lano)
- **UK**: R (on the application of Lumba) v Secretary of State for the Home Department [2011] UKSC 12

#### Family Reunification
- **EU**: C-540/03 (Parliament v Council), C-133/15 (Chavez-Vilchez)
- **Germany**: BVerfG, 1 BvR 2222/12 (Family unity)

#### Non-Refoulement
- **ECtHR**: Soering v. United Kingdom (1989), M.S.S. v. Belgium and Greece (2011)
- **CJEU**: C-391/16, C-77/17, C-297/17 (Afghanistan cases)

## Verification Checklist

For each legal claim, verify:
- [ ] Primary source (official government/EU document)
- [ ] Date of document (current within 2 years)
- [ ] Jurisdiction (which country/region it applies to)
- [ ] Effective date (when it came into force)
- [ ] Any amendments or updates
- [ ] Cross-reference with 2+ independent sources
- [ ] Expert review (if complex legal issue)
