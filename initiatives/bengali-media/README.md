# Bengali Media & Culture Initiatives

## Overview
Digital media initiatives preserving and promoting Bengali language, culture, and community engagement through accessible audio content.

## Current Ideas (22-25)
- **22**: Village Story Pods - Mobile recording booths for oral histories
- **23**: Bengali Audiobook Co-op - Crowdsourced audiobook creation
- **24**: Radio Drama for Social Change - Serialized dramas on taboo topics
- **25**: Folk Music Licensing Collective - Platform for indie musicians

## Implementation Status
- **Phase 1 (Validation)**: Weeks 1-4 - Testing Village Story Pods
- **Phase 2 (Pilot)**: Weeks 5-12 - Launching Radio Drama series
- **Phase 3 (Scale)**: Months 4-6 - Expanding to Tripura + diaspora

## Key Contacts
- Local NGOs in Nadia, Murshidabad, Birbhum districts
- Bengali theatre groups
- Community radio stations

## Resources
- Technical: AzuraCast, Icecast, WordPress, Audacity
- Legal: MIB regulations, copyright law
- Financial: Cultural grants, sponsorships
