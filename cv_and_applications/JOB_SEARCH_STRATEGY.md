## Application Template (Active Voice, Human)

**Subject line formula:** `[Your name] — [Role] — [Key differentiator]`
Example: `Sourov Deb — Business English Trainer — CELTA + 11 years luxury hospitality`

**Email body (3 paragraphs max):**
1. What you want and why them (specific, one sentence each)
2. What you bring that others don't (your multilingual + CELTA + hospitality combination is rare)
3. What you are asking for (a call, a trial lesson, a CV review)

**Do not:**
- Write more than 200 words
- List everything on your CV
- Use passive voice ("I was responsible for" → "I managed")
