## Key Writing Principles

1. **Active voice always.** Not "mistakes were made" — "I made mistakes."
2. **Human, not monotone.** Vary sentence length. Let the pace breathe.
3. **Quality over quantity.** One honest paragraph beats five polished ones.
4. **Sources from official, legal, tested places only.**
5. **Protect the rhythm.** 500 words/day beats nothing.
