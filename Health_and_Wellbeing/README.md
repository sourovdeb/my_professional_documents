## 🔍 Early Warning Signs (Prodromal Symptoms)

Learn to recognize when you're moving into:
- **Hypomania/Mania**: Racing thoughts, decreased need for sleep, increased spending
- **Depression**: Withdrawing, decreased motivation, sleep changes, concentration issues
- **Mixed state**: Depressed mood + racing thoughts (highest suicide risk)

**Track in**: `Symptom_Log.md`

```
Date: 2026-06-03
Symptom: Racing thoughts
Severity: 4/10
Possible trigger: Caffeine after 2pm
Action taken: Cut off caffeine, called therapist
```

## 🎯 Wellness Habits

### Sleep (Most Important for Bipolar)
- ✅ Aim for consistent bedtime (even weekends)
- ✅ Same wake time
- ✅ 7-9 hours nightly
- ✅ No screens 30 min before bed
- ✅ Track: sleep app or checklist

### Movement
- ✅ 20-30 min daily (walk, stretch, yoga)
- ✅ Helps mood, sleep, energy
- ✅ No need for gym; walking counts
- ✅ Track: app or "yes/no" checklist

### Nutrition
- ✅ Eat regularly (3 meals + snacks)
- ✅ Limit caffeine (can trigger mania)
- ✅ Stay hydrated
- ✅ Limit sugar and alcohol
- ✅ Track: simple checklist (ate 3 meals?)

### Social Connection
- ✅ One person contact daily (text, call, visit)
- ✅ Reach out when depressed (hardest time)
- ✅ Join group (writing group, therapy group, online community)
- ✅ Track: who did you connect with?

### Stress Management
- ✅ 10-15 min meditation or breathing
- ✅ Journaling (process emotions)
- ✅ Creative outlet (writing, art, music)
- ✅ Hot bath or shower
- ✅ Track: what helped today?

## 🚨 Crisis Plan

If you're struggling:

**During depressive episode**:
1. Don't isolate—reach out (therapist, crisis line, trusted friend)
2. Lower expectations (500 words → 100 words okay)
3. Keep meds on schedule
4. Maintain basic self-care (sleep, eat, move)
5. Crisis line: [your local number]

**During manic/hypomanic episode**:
1. Reduce stimulation (limit social media, meetings)
2. Sleep is critical—doctor may adjust meds
3. Avoid major decisions
4. Tell someone you trust what's happening
5. Document ideas (don't act on them yet)

**File**: `Crisis_Contacts.md` (numbers, therapist, crisis lines)

## 🎯 Productivity + Mental Health Balance

**Key principle**: You're not broken. Your brain works differently.

This means:
- ✅ Some days you write 500 words easily
- ✅ Some days 100 words is a win
- ✅ Some weeks you focus on health, not writing
- ✅ It's all progress

**Writing schedule adapts to your energy**:
- High energy period: write more, reach out for partnerships, job search
- Stable period: consistent daily writing, maintain habits
- Low energy period: shorter entries, focus on meds/therapy, self-care
