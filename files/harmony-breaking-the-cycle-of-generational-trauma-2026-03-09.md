### 1. The Paradox: "Conditionally Competent"

four languages, I am a sommelier, I have supervised teams, and I've traveled the globe [1, 2]. But there is a
massive contradiction in my life. I can win "Sprints"—discrete, high-stakes tasks like exams—because they provide
an **unassailable identity** and external validation [3, 4]. However, I fail at "Marathons." It took me seven years to
finish a four-year degree, and I often sideline myself into assistant roles because the **"Fear of Being Seen"** in a
position of ultimate authority feels like a life-threat [5-7].
### 2. My "Baked" Identity (Childhood)
Psychologically, I wasn't just "damaged" by events; I was **"baked"** in a home of chronic tension [8, 9]. My parents were
the **"North and South Poles"**—an unhappy arranged marriage between a depressed, physically punitive mother and
an emotionally invisible, stubborn father [10-12]. As the first child, I was the **"silent sponge"** who "took the hit" for
their drama [10, 12, 13].
To survive, I practiced **Structural Dissociation**. I split into two:
* **The Apparently Normal Part (ANP):** The "little adult" who was polite and achieved things to keep the peace [14,
15].
* **The Emotional Part (EP):** The child who "unscrewed his head from his body" to escape into books while being
abused by a neighbor [16, 17].
### 3. The "Freeze" is my Bodyguard
My biggest problem today is **Executive Paralysis**. When I have to do something "formal" where I'll be judged by an
authority figure, my system doesn't just get "lazy"—it enters **Dorsal Vagal Shutdown** [18, 19]. My brain becomes a
**"Turtle,"** pulling its head in to conserve energy because it believes that visibility equals danger [20, 21]. I am stuck
in a loop where my body sees **"doing nothing" as the safest possible state** [22].
### 4. The Shadow and the "Bag"
I carry a **"Long Bag"** behind me full of things I was told were "bad"—anger, greed, power, and even my own brilliance
[23, 24]. My "vices"—being a **"greedy liar, a predator, or a controller"**—are actually **"Protector" parts** [25, 26].
