## Productivity Strategies for Bipolar / Depression

### The No-Zero Day Rule
Do at least one small thing every day, no matter how small.
Writing one sentence = not a zero day. One minute of exercise = not a zero day.

### Energy-Aware Scheduling
| Energy State | What to do |
|-------------|------------|
| **High energy** | Write, create, record, plan |
| **Moderate** | Edit, reply to emails, admin tasks |
| **Low energy** | Read, voice-type, watch tutorials, rest |
| **Very low** | Protect sleep. Do nothing. It’s enough. |

### Reduce Decision Fatigue
- Write at the **same time every day** (anchor habit)
- Keep a **post template** — structure is already decided for you
- Use **auto-suggest** in the publisher app — no manual tagging
- Prepare the next day’s topic **the night before**

### Voice-to-Text When Energy is Low
- **Google Docs** has built-in voice typing: Tools > Voice Typing
- **Whisper.cpp** (local, private): `whisper audio.wav --model small --output-format txt`
- **Otter.ai** (free tier): real-time transcription, meeting notes
