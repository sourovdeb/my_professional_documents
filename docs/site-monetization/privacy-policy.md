# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** [[LAST UPDATED]]

This Privacy Policy explains how sourovdeb.com ("the Site", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you visit. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and French data protection law (as La Réunion is part of the European Union).

## 1. Who we are (Data Controller)

The data controller responsible for this Site is **[[SITE OWNER NAME]]**, based in **[[LOCATION]]**. For any privacy question or to exercise your rights, contact us at **[[CONTACT EMAIL]]**.

## 2. What data we collect

We collect the minimum data needed to run the Site:

- **Information you give us voluntarily** — for example, your name and email if you use a contact form or leave a comment.
- **Technical data collected automatically** — IP address (anonymised where possible), browser type, device type, operating system, referring page, and pages visited. This is standard server and analytics data.
- **Cookies and similar technologies** — see Section 6.

We do **not** knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

## 3. How we use your data

We use your data only to:

- Respond to messages you send us
- Display and moderate comments (if enabled)
- Understand how the Site is used, so we can improve content (analytics)
- Serve and measure advertising (see Section 7)
- Keep the Site secure and prevent abuse

## 4. Legal basis for processing (GDPR Article 6)

- **Consent** — for non-essential cookies, analytics, and personalised advertising. You give this via the cookie banner and can withdraw it at any time.
- **Legitimate interest** — for basic security, spam prevention, and aggregated, non-identifying usage statistics.
- **Contract / legitimate interest** — to reply when you contact us.

## 5. How long we keep your data

- Contact messages: kept only as long as needed to handle your request, then deleted.
- Comments: kept while the post is published, unless you ask for removal.
- Analytics data: retained in aggregated form per the analytics provider's default retention.

## 6. Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device. We use:

- **Essential cookies** — required for the Site to function (no consent needed).
- **Analytics cookies** — help us understand traffic (consent required).
- **Advertising cookies** — used by Google AdSense and its partners to serve relevant ads (consent required).

When you first visit, a **cookie consent banner** lets you accept or reject non-essential cookies. You can change your choice at any time via the cookie settings link in the footer. Blocking cookies may affect some features.

## 7. Advertising — Google AdSense

This Site displays ads through **Google AdSense** to keep all content free to read.

- Google and its partners use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.
- Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits to this Site and/or other sites on the Internet.
- You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting **Google Ads Settings** (adssettings.google.com), or opt out of third-party vendor cookies at **www.aboutads.info**.
- For EU users, ads are served in line with Google's EU user consent policy — you consent (or not) via our cookie banner.

More information on how Google uses data is available at **policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites**.

## 8. Third-party services

Besides Google AdSense, this Site may use:

- **Analytics** (e.g. Google Analytics or a privacy-friendly alternative) — to measure traffic.
- **WordPress / hosting (Hostinger)** — which processes server logs to run the Site.
- **Embedded content** (e.g. YouTube, images) — which may set its own cookies.

Each third party has its own privacy policy; we do not control their processing.

## 9. Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

- **Access** the data we hold about you
- **Rectify** inaccurate data
- **Erase** your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restrict** or **object** to processing
- **Data portability** — receive your data in a portable format
- **Withdraw consent** at any time
- **Lodge a complaint** with a supervisory authority — in France, the **CNIL** (www.cnil.fr)

To exercise any right, email **[[CONTACT EMAIL]]**. We will respond within one month.

## 10. Data security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures (HTTPS encryption, access controls, up-to-date software) to protect your data. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, but we take protection seriously.

## 11. International transfers

Some providers (e.g. Google) may process data outside the EU. Where they do, they rely on legal safeguards such as the EU–US Data Privacy Framework or Standard Contractual Clauses.

## 12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top shows the latest version. Significant changes will be flagged on the Site.

## 13. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email **[[CONTACT EMAIL]]**.
