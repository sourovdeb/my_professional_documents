# Migration Law Microblog - New Ideas (Deep Brainstorm)

## Frame
- Subject: Legal information dissemination for migrants
- Goal: Timely, accurate, accessible legal information
- Constraints: Legal accuracy, multilingual requirements, telecom regulations, liability
- Horizons: Immediate (3m), 1 year, 5+ years
- Success: User adoption, accuracy rating, impact on migrant outcomes, scalability

## Perspective Rotation (Top Idea: Legal Change SMS)
- End User (Migrant): Needs timely info, may have limited internet, language barriers, fear of authorities
- Adversary (Government agency): May want to control information flow, prevent panic
- Regulator (Telecom authority): Requires opt-in compliance, anti-spam laws, data protection
- Financier (NGO): Wants measurable impact on migrant outcomes, cost-effectiveness
- Operator: Needs reliable legal sources, SMS costs, user management

## Second-Order Effects
- Short-term: Migrants receive timely legal updates, reduced information asymmetry
- Long-term: Reduced exploitation of migrants, increased trust in legal system
- Then: Migrants make more informed decisions about their status
- And then: Policy improvements based on identified gaps and needs
